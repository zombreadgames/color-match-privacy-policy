DELETE ACCOUNT

Effective Date: August 17, 2025.

Last Updated: August 17, 2025.

1. What data we collect

When you sign in with Google Play Games, we may receive only your public profile information provided by Google:

  • Google Play Games ID (unique identifier)
  • Public display name (nickname)
  • Public profile image

From these, we only use and store your Google Play Games ID to create and manage your player account. Your public name and image are not required for gameplay and are not stored in our database.

2. Game data & retention

Your game data (e.g., progress, settings, items) is saved in our database and linked to your Google Play Games ID. We keep this data until you request deletion.

3. How to request account & data deletion

In-app (recommended): open the User Profile screen (the same screen where you change your nickname) and tap the trash icon button located on the right side of the menu on that screen. Follow the confirmation prompts to permanently delete your account and data.

Once deletion is complete, your player account and all game data linked to your Google Play Games ID will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. This includes your progress, items, and any purchases or paid entitlements associated with the account. Proceed with caution.

4. Irreversibility

Deletion is permanent. If you play again after deletion, a new account may be created when you sign in with Google Play Games.
